HOW TO PLAY

Each level has a collection of colour pairs. For every click a box flips over to reveal colour. Match all the pairs with as few clicks as possible.You will have a moment to memorise the cards after the play button is clicked. Keep your focus, a moment could be milliseconds. Once a box is flipped open, that pair will need to be colour matched before a new colour can be flipped open. There is a maximum number of clicks allowed per level. Unused clicks on each level are automatically added to the allocated number of clicks for the next level. The fewer clicks used, the more clicks can be saved up for higher levels. Enjoy your game!

Game stats
Click Count

This indicates the number of clicks you have made, be careful not to be trigger-happy.

Clicks Left

You are given bonus clicks for each level. At the start of the game you get 6 clicks, an additional 12 clicks in level 2, an additional 24 clicks in level 3 and an additional 48 clicks in level 4.

Current Score

This score represents your progress in the ongoing game session, you get 5 points for every coloured box open. A wrong click deducts 1 point.

Best Score

This score represents the highest score you have ever achieved in the game. Aim to increase this number.
